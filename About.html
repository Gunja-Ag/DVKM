  Doon Valley Knitting Mills

Suppliers to D. G. S. & D. , Railway, Para Military, State Police

Doon Valley Knitting Mills is subject to Dehradun Jurisdiction Small Scale Unit. We are manufacturers and suppliers registered with D. G. S. & D. , India. We are also registered with many other departments & companies. Our goal is to provide our customers with quality product by carefully planning and monitering the product, from the starting till the end.

  • Re-designing and customization of your uniform
  • High-quality product and customer satisfaction guaranteed
  • Quick and efficient services provided


We are acclaimed manufacturers and suppliers of all kinds of woollen hosiery and textile goods. We are known for our quality products at competetive prices. We supply our products to Police, Paramilitary & Military & other government departments to their full satisfaction and above and beyond their expectations.

We have an in-house fully equipped laboratory in the factory premises itself to maintain quality control at every step of the manufacturing process. Our commitment to quality is such that we even refuse to take orders from customers, if we are asked to lower the quality of the product to reduce the price. Our brand name D. V. K. M. has a value and a reputation in the market and we do our best to keep that brand name. Our team is working hard everyday to make this brand name better.

We have sufficient capacity to supply bulk quantities in time. We have been supplying to government departments in huge quantities for a long time now and so we have rich experience in handling such orders.
